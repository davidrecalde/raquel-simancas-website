04Quick answers
Are you taking new PhD students?

Selectively, yes — particularly for projects in CO2 valorization and methane upgrading. Please send a CV, a 1-page research statement, and indicate funding interests.

Do you accept industrial collaborations?

Yes. Formal sponsored research, joint development, technical advisory and licensing of group IP are all viable. Reach out via the Collaborations channel.

Can you give a keynote?

Schedule permitting — yes. Please share venue, audience, date and intended scope. Topics: zeolite science, sustainable catalysis, CO2 chemistry, women in materials science.
