02Research areas

Six lines of work, one ambition — more sustainable chemistry.

Our group designs zeolite materials and catalytic systems with precise control of matter at the molecular scale — and translates them to industrial-relevant chemistries: carbon valorization, methane upgrading, and clean processes.
